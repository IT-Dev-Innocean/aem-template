All-New SANTA FE XRT: A Blend of Toughness and Premium Comfort for Every Journey

 

As the main highlight for Hyundai at IIMS 2026, the All-New SANTA FE XRT arrives as the most premium variant with the boldest and toughest character in the All-New SANTA FE line. Carrying the spirit of“Adventure with Class”, this SUV is designed for urban professionals and modern families who desire premium comfort alongside real capability for various travel needs.

 

The All-New SANTA FE XRT displays visual and functional improvements that emphasize a tougher SUV character. This is visible in the use of20-inch alloy wheels specific to the XRT, side steps, cross bar dan mud guards that not only strengthen the look but also support ease of access and protection when driving in various conditions. Additional details such as metal pedals further emphasize the premium yet functional impression typical of the XRT.

 

Inside the cabin, the All-New SANTA FE XRT offers an interior experience that is more premium and comfort-oriented. This variant is equipped with premium relaxation seats, electric captain seats in the second row, and a panoramic sunroof that creates a sense of wider and more exclusive space. The presence of dual wireless charging and an integrated entertainment system further supports comfort and ease of use, especially for consumers with high activity levels.

 

In terms of technology and safety, the All-New SANTA FE XRT is equipped with a suite of features designed to support active mobility with a higher level of comfort and safety. This variant is equipped with Hyundai SmartSense with active safety features and driver assistance, as well as Hyundai Bluelink integrated with Emergency Roadside Assistance (RSA). The presence of a BOSE Premium Sound System also completes the more refined and classy driving experience.

 

Powered by a 1.6 Turbo Hybrid engine, the All-New SANTA FE XRT offers a balance of performance and efficiency relevant to modern mobility needs. With improvements in design, comfort, and technology aspects, the All-New SANTA FE XRT becomes the All-New SANTA FE variant with the most complete character, designed for those who live an urban life but still desire the flexibility to step further beyond routine.

 

NeNew CRETA Alpha: Bolder Design and More Complete Features for Daily Mobility

 

Complementing the launch of the All-New SANTA FE XRT, Hyundai also presents the New CRETA Alpha, which was previously launched simultaneously in several cities earlier this year and received a positive reception from the public. Now, this model is brought to IIMS 2026 to further complete the visitor experience, with its new bold design, premium character, and modern features that support both daily mobility and long-distance travel.

 

With the distinct Alpha visual identity, black treatment touches on the exterior, as well as interior updates and features such as the electric power tailgate, Hyundai SmartSense, and Bluelink, connectivity, the New CRETA Alpha offers a compact SUV that is stylish, confident, and ready to accompany a dynamic urban lifestyle.
