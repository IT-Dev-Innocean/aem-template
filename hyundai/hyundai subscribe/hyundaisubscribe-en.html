• Hyundai launches Hyundai Subscribe, the first EV subscription service in the Asia-Pacific region for Hyundai
  • Hyundai Subscribe offers subscription schemes of up to 60 months with full-service benefits

 

Jakarta, 11 February 2026 – PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID) is participating in the Indonesia International Motor Show (IIMS) held at Hall D JIEXPO Kemayoran from 5-15 February 2026. On this occasion, Hyundai launched Hyundai Subscribe, a monthly electric vehicle subscription program that delivers a worry-free driving experience. With monthly fees that cover insurance, maintenance, and taxes, customers can enjoy the future of mobility in a more convenient and flexible way.

 

“We aim to strengthen our premium EV presence while continuing to prioritize accessibility. Hyundai Subscribe offers a flexible way for our customers to enjoy our latest technology without the limitations of traditional ownership. I am proud to announce that specifically for Hyundai, this program is being introduced for the first time in the Asia-Pacific region” said Kenny Lee, President Director of PT Hyundai Solusi Mobilitas.

 

The introduction of Hyundai Subscribe offers a vehicle ownership solution that is more relevant to the needs of Indonesian consumers today. Through this program, customers can enjoy Hyundai vehicles through a practical scheme without long term commitment. This initiative also reflects our commitment to driving EV adoption in Indonesia, while delivering a mobility experience that is easier, more transparent, and comprehensive.

 

What is Hyundai Subscribe and How Can This Program Become a Mobility Solution?

 

Recent data shows a significant shift in Indonesian consumers’ interest toward vehicle subscription models, increasing from 49 percent to 72 percent recent years. Consumers are increasingly seeking convenience, vehicle availability, and greater flexibility, reflecting a preference for more practical and adaptive mobility solutions. In response to this trend, Hyundai Subscribe is designed with flexibility at its core, delivering a seamless and customer focused mobility experience.

 

In line with Hyundai’s commitment to delivering relevant innovation, Hyundai Subscribe comes as a subscription solution with fixed monthly costs, providing certainty and comfort in vehicle usage.

 

In its initial phase, Hyundai Subscribe offers IONIQ 5 and IONIQ 5 N as electric vehicle options under the subscription scheme, complete with comprehensive service benefits including maintenance, insurance and tax, roadside assistance, and EV support services. Hyundai provides subscription periods ranging from 12 to 60 months.

 

This program also provides greater accessibility for consumers to experience Hyundai’s premium EV lineup at a more affordable cost, without the need for significant upfront investment typically associated with conventional vehicle ownership. At the same time, it sets a new benchmark for premium EV accessibility and flexibility, redefining how Indonesian consumers can access and enjoy Hyundai’s electric vehicles.

 

Here’s a closer look at the standout strengths behind Hyundai’s premium EV lineup:

 

IONIQ 5 is Hyundai’s EV lineup built on the dedicated Electric Global Modular Platform (E-GMP), offering fast charging capability along with innovative features such as Vehicle to Load (V2L) functionality, Bluelink connectivity, and a spacious interior with premium comfort. Designed for those seeking a smart, stylish, and clean mobility solution, IONIQ 5 combines sustainability with everyday practicality and modern innovation.

 

IONIQ 5 N is the high-performance version of the IONIQ 5, delivering up to 478 kW (650 PS) of power from its dual motor system, accelerating from 0 to 100 km per hour in approximately 3.4 seconds and reaching a top speed of a round 260 km per hour. Equipped with an 84 kWh battery and performance focused driving technologies, this model is ideal for those who seek strong performance, sporty design, and a more exhilarating driving experience.

 

How to Subscribe to Hyundai Subscribe?

 

  • Choose your preferred IONIQ model
  • Choose the subscription period
  • Complete the registration data
  • Enjoy a worry-free electric driving experience

 

Hyundai Subscribe also offers a hassle-free driving experience. Customers are not required to pay significant upfront costs and can enjoy additional benefits, including the availability of a replacement vehicle in emergency situations, 24-hour customer service, and complimentary maintenance and insurance coverage, allowing users to experience more flexible and comfortable mobility in line with modern lifestyles.

 

For more information about Hyundai Subscribe, visit Hyundai Solusi Mobilitas.
