• STARGAZER sales grew 13.3% YoY, helping Hyundai secure third position in the B-MPV segment
  • The presence of STARGAZER Cartenz & Cartenz X has become one of the key contributors to Hyundai’s performance in this segment
  • Hyundai is offering various special promotions throughout April 2026, along with the opportunity to win a free trip to watch the FIFA World Cup 2026™ in Los Angeles

 

Jakarta, 28 April 2026 – PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID) announced Hyundai’s positive achievement in the B-MPV segment. As of March 2026 year-to-month, Hyundai secured third position with a market share of 8.5%. This performance was driven by Hyundai STARGAZER sales growth of 13.3% year-on-year, as well as a 2.3 percentage point increase in market share compared to the same period last year.

 

This achievement reinforces Hyundai’s position in the B-MPV segment while reflecting stronger consumer acceptance of STARGAZER as a modern and innovative MPV choice for Indonesian families.

 

Kenny Lee, President Director of PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia, said, “This achievement shows that Hyundai continues to strengthen its position in the highly competitive B-MPV segment. The presence of models such as STARGAZER Cartenz and Cartenz X has supported positive growth momentum in the early part of this year by offering features that are relevant to the needs of modern families, such as Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go and Hyundai Bluelink, which support a more comfortable, safe, and connected driving experience. We will continue to drive sustainable growth through a strategy focused on the needs of Indonesian consumers.”

 

“STARGAZER’s contribution has been key in driving this growth, supported by a combination of performance, comfort, and safety features that are relevant to consumer needs. Beyond the product itself, we continue to enhance added value through aftersales services such as myHyundaiCare, which supports customers from the purchase process, throughout ownership, and up to vehicle upgrade, including benefits such as Resale Value Guarantee and Extended Warranty to provide greater long-term peace of mind. Moving forward, we will continue to strengthen the competitiveness of our products and services to deliver a more comprehensive experience in the B-MPV segment,” said Fransiscus Soerjopranoto, Chief Operating Officer of PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia.

 

What Makes STARGAZER Cartenz and Cartenz X Stand Out in the B-MPV Segment?

 

The factors that strengthen STARGAZER’s competitiveness in this segment are also reflected in the presence of STARGAZER Cartenz and Cartenz X, which combine performance, comfort, connectivity, and safety to support a more complete driving experience. In terms of performance, both models offer a smooth and efficient driving character supported by Smooth Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT), making them comfortable for both daily mobility and long-distance journeys. The driving experience is also made more practical through Built-in Navigation and Hyundai Bluelink, which help customers stay connected to their vehicle and access information support throughout their journey.

 

Cabin comfort is also enhanced through the combination of Captain Seats, which provides a more personal and premium second-row seating experience, as well as Driver Ventilated Seat* to help keep the driver cool, especially in hot weather conditions. Meanwhile, Hyundai SmartSense** equips the vehicle with active safety features, including Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go**, which helps support driver comfort and awareness, particularly when driving in dynamic traffic conditions. In STARGAZER X, the driving experience is also supported by Blind-spot View Monitor* and Surround View Monitor*, providing more optimal visibility around the vehicle.

 

“During my time using STARGAZER, I have experienced a smooth and less tiring drive, especially on long-distance trips. The cabin is also spacious and comfortable for family use. In terms of safety, Hyundai SmartSense, with features such as Lane Following Assist, Lane Keeping Assist, and Cruise Control, is very helpful and makes me feel safer throughout the journey. Overall, I highly recommend STARGAZER for those looking for a car that is both comfortable and safe for various needs,” said Mulyadi M, Head of the Hyundai STARGAZER Owner Indonesia Community.
