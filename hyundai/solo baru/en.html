• PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID) continues to strengthen its nationwide after-sales service network as part of its commitment to providing a comprehensive vehicle ownership experience for customers.
  • This commitment is demonstrated through the opening of Hyundai Authorized Body & Paint Solo Baru by Hyundai Solo Baru, offering officially authorized body repair and paint services that are easily accessible to customers in Solo and surrounding areas.
  • Hyundai Authorized Body & Paint Solo Baru is also equipped with the Hyundai Premium Courtesy Car service to ensure customer comfort and mobility during vehicle repairs.

 

Solo, June 9, 2026 – PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID) has once again reaffirmed its commitment to delivering a comprehensive vehicle ownership experience. Together with PT Bintang Asia Global, HMID officially inaugurated Hyundai Authorized Body & Paint Solo Baru. The presence of this facility marks a strategic step by Hyundai in strengthening its service ecosystem in the Greater Solo Raya, in line with growing mobility demands and increasing customer needs for authorized after-sales services.

 

Located at Jl. Ir. Soekarno RT.5/RW.4 (The Park Area), Dusun II, Madegondo, Grogol District, Sukoharjo Regency, Central Java, Hyundai Authorized Body & Paint Solo Baru is situated within the same integrated complex as the Hyundai showroom and authorized service center. This provides Hyundai customers in Solo and surrounding areas with convenient access to comprehensive services, from vehicle purchases to after-sales support.

 

Fransiscus Soerjopranoto, Chief Operating Officer of PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID), said, "Hyundai is steadfast in its commitment to delivering innovation, spanning from product offerings to aftersales services. The launch of Body & Paint services in Solo Raya, complemented by the Hyundai Premium Courtesy Car program, embodies the spirit of myHyundaiCare and represents a concrete step in providing aftersales solutions that are closer, more convenient, and more comprehensive for our valued customers. Furthermore, we plan to progressively expand our aftersales network to keep pace with the significantly growing customer base"

 

Customers of Hyundai Authorized Body & Paint are entitled to enjoy the Hyundai Premium Courtesy Car service if the repair process requires more than 14 days. This service applies to all vehicles distributed by PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia, whether still under warranty or no longer covered by warranty, subject to vehicle availability at the dealership.

 

The Body & Paint facility is supported by highly skilled and certified technicians, complete and standardized equipment, genuine Hyundai parts, repair materials and paint that meet Hyundai standards, as well as a final inspection process to ensure optimal workmanship quality and repair results.

 

In addition, if customers have time constraints and cannot go to the authorized service center, Hyundai Authorize Body & Paint Solo Baru also offers vehicle pick-up and delivery services.

 

Ronny Lukito, Director of PT Bintang Asia Global, stated, "With the growing population of Hyundai vehicles in Solo Raya, the presence of Hyundai Authorized Body & Paint Solo Baru further strengthens our commitment to delivering the best service to customers, not only in terms of sales but also through comprehensive, high-standard after-sales support."

 

Currently, there are 8 Hyundai dealerships across Indonesia that offer Body & Paint services. With the addition of Body & Paint services at various Hyundai dealerships, including Solo Baru, HMID reaffirms its commitment to consistently providing the highest service standards for customers throughout Indonesia. Hyundai will continue to introduce innovations and accessible facilities, ensuring that every customer enjoys a comprehensive and high-quality vehicle ownership experience.
