• Hyundai presents innovative micromobility solutions through the E3W (Electric 3 Wheel) and E4W (Micro Electric 4 Wheel) concept display at Hyundai Motorstudio Senayan Park (HMS).
  • To build relevance with younger generations, Hyundai Motorstudio Senayan Park also features an interactive Game Zone with Asphalt Legends Unite and Roblox.

 

Jakarta, March 3rd 2026 – Hyundai Motor Company reaffirms its commitment to Progress for Humanity with the launch of HMS Micro Mobility X Game Zone “Elevate for the Future” at Hyundai Motorstudio (HMS) Senayan Park. HMS is a space where anyone can freely experience Hyundai’s clean mobility vision along with a variety of activities and engaging environments. This interactive showcase combines a forward-looking of the Electric 3 Wheel (E3W) and Micro Electric 4 Wheel (E4W) concepts with an immersive digital gaming experience.

 

 

Kenny Lee, President Director of Hyundai Motors Indonesia, stated that, “Hyundai Motorstudio Senayan Park reflects our vision of bringing sustainability and innovation closer to the public. Much like in gaming, mobility is constantly evolving. By showcasing our E3W and E4W concepts alongside an interactive Game Zone featuring titles such as Asphalt Legends Unite and Roblox, we demonstrate the next level of clean urban transport while connecting meaningfully with younger generations. This space is where innovation, culture, and sustainability converge to unlock new possibilities for the future.”

 

The “Elevate for the Future” activation highlights the potential for compact, electrified solutions designed to address modern city challenges through two main attractions:

 

  • Studio – Future/Smart Mobility

    The exhibition features the E3W and E4W concepts, developed in collaboration with TVS Motor and first unveiled at Bharat Mobility Global Expo 2025. These vehicles are engineered to reduce air pollution and navigate dense cityscapes. These are versatile EV with foldable seats for wheelchair users, adjustable body height, and stable performance even in heavy monsoon conditions and can be optimized for “last-mile” connectivity. Visitors can explore these units up close, watch a dedicated concept film, and discover how micro-mobility can create quieter, more environmentally friendly urban environments.

  • Lounge – Gaming Mobility

    To complement the vehicle showcase, the HMS Game Zone merges clean mobility innovation with interactive entertainment. This zone emphasizes competition and performance, allowing visitors to experience the sensation of “levelling up” in alignment with Hyundai’s spirit of continuous innovation.

    • Collaborations: Features global titles including Asphalt Legends Unite and Roblox.
    • Interactive Engagement: Visitors who share photos of the display units on Instagram gain the opportunity to play, collect coins, and win exclusive prizes.

 

 

This integrated experience strengthens Hyundai’s relevance among younger audiences by presenting sustainable solutions through an engaging, educational lens.

 

Experience the future of mobility at the HMS Micro Mobility Exhibition & Game Zone at Senayan Park starting March 3, 2026.
