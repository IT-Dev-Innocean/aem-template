Jakarta, 22 May 2026 – Fuel expenses often remain one of the most difficult costs to manage for families who rely on vehicles for their daily mobility. Amid global economic uncertainty that continues to affect the purchasing power of Indonesian consumers, household expense management has become increasingly important. It is therefore unsurprising that fuel efficiency has now become one of the key considerations when choosing a family vehicle, alongside comfort and passenger capacity.

 

Within the MPV segment itself, this challenge is even more apparent: vehicles with larger passenger capacity tend to consume more fuel, making the choice of engine technology a critical factor that cannot be overlooked. Hyundai addresses these needs through the Hyundai STARGAZER Cartenz, designed to support the diverse mobility needs of Indonesian families, from daily activities to long-distance travel.

 

What Are the Key Features of Hyundai STARGAZER Cartenz for Indonesian Families?

The Hyundai STARGAZER Cartenz is a 7-seater MPV that combines modern design, a spacious cabin with Captain Seat configuration options, and fuel efficiency in one competitive package. Powered by the Smartstream G1.5 MPI 1.5L inline 4-cylinder engine delivering 115 PS and 144 Nm of torque, the vehicle is designed to handle various driving conditions in Indonesia efficiently.

 

Below are the key features that make the Hyundai STARGAZER Cartenz relevant for the needs of Indonesian families:

  • Hyundai SmartSense: An active safety system available on the Smart HSS and Prime HSS variants, featuring Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist, Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist, and Lane Keeping Assist.
  • Smart Cruise Control (SCC) with Stop & Go: Helps maintain speed and safe following distance automatically while driving in heavy traffic conditions. This feature is available on the Smart HSS and Prime HSS variants.
  • Flexible Captain Seat configuration: Available on the Style, Smart HSS, and Prime HSS trims, delivering enhanced comfort for every family member throughout the journey.
  • Built-in Navigation: Navigation features integrated directly into the vehicle’s infotainment system and usable without an active internet connection, available on the Smart HSS and Prime HSS variants.
  • Parking Distance Warning: Available exclusively on the Prime HSS variant, this feature provides alerts for surrounding objects while parking or maneuvering the vehicle.
  • Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS): Monitors tire pressure in real time through the vehicle cluster for greater driving safety, available exclusively on the Smart HSS and Prime HSS variants.

 

The Hyundai STARGAZER Cartenz is available starting from IDR 276,100,000 (OTR Jakarta). Prices and specifications are subject to change without prior notice; customers are advised to verify the latest information through authorized Hyundai dealers.
