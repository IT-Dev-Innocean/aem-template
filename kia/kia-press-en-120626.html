  • ● The all-new Carens and The new Sonet take center stage, offering visitors the opportunity to explore Kia’s latest products firsthand.
  • ● Kia Total Care provides added peace of mind with 4-Year Free Service and a 7-Year/200,000 km Warranty for The all-new Carens and The new Sonet.

 

JAKARTA, 11 June 2026 – Kia participates in Jakarta Fair 2026, taking place at Hall A1 No.16, JIExpo Kemayoran, Jakarta, from June 11 to July 12. As one of Indonesia’s largest and most anticipated annual exhibitions, Jakarta Fair provides Kia with an opportunity to showcase The all-new Carens and The new Sonet, while offering visitors access to various promotional programs throughout the event.

Albertus Welly Nugroho, VP Commercial Kia Sales Indonesia, said, “Jakarta Fair has always been one of the most accessible and popular events for the public, bringing together a wide range of products and services in one location. Through our participation, we want to provide more opportunities for visitors to experience Kia firsthand, explore the key features of our vehicles, and learn more about the quality of our products and services.”

At Jakarta Fair 2026, Kia highlights The all-new Carens and The new Sonet as its featured models. Both vehicles are designed to address the evolving mobility needs of Indonesian consumers.

The all-new Carens offers a combination of comfort, flexibility, and practicality designed to support family journeys. Featuring a functional cabin configuration across all rows and a range of comfort-oriented features, The all-new Carens is well-suited for consumers seeking a vehicle that can accommodate both daily activities and long-distance travel.

Meanwhile, The new Sonet is a compact SUV that combines dynamic styling with a range of modern features. Its agile driving characteristics and city-friendly dimensions make it a practical choice for consumers looking for a vehicle that supports everyday urban mobility.

  • ● Trendy: Rp299,000,000
  • ● Motion: Rp339,000,000
  • ● Signature: Rp379,000,000

The Jakarta OTR prices for The new Sonet are as follows:

  • ● Trendy: Rp263,000,000
  • ● Motion: Rp288,000,000
  • ● Signature: Rp313,000,000

Kia is also offering the Kia Total Care program, designed to provide greater peace of mind throughout the ownership journey. The program reflects Kia’s commitment to delivering a more reassuring ownership experience while strengthening customer confidence in the quality of its products and aftersales services.

Through Kia Total Care, customers purchasing The all-new Carens and The new Sonet can enjoy:

  • ● 4 Years Free Service*
  • ● 7 Years / 200,000 km Warranty*

In addition, customers can place orders for other Kia models, including The Kia Carnival and The Kia EV9 GT-Line. Kia also offers a range of additional aftersales benefits, including:

  • ● 5 Years / 150,000 km Warranty: Vehicle warranty coverage for up to five years or 150,000 km (applicable to The Kia Carnival and The Kia EV9 GT-Line)*
  • ● 12-Months / 20,000 km Parts Warranty: Parts warranty coverage for up to 12 months or 20,000 km*
  • ● 8 Years / 160,000 km Battery Warranty: Battery warranty coverage for up to eight years or 160,000 km (applicable to EV and Hybrid models)*

Through its participation in Jakarta Fair 2026, Kia aims to provide more consumers with the opportunity to explore its products firsthand and obtain information tailored to their mobility needs.
