• ● Kia Spazio Tower officially opens as a strategic dealership in West Surabaya.
  • ● Strengthening customer accessibility through integrated facilities that bring the Kia ownership experience closer to customers in East Java.
  • ● Reaffirming Kia’s strategy in East Java through network expansion, sales growth, and stronger aftersales services and ownership experience.

 

Surabaya, 12 May 2026 – Kia continues to strengthen its expansion strategy in Indonesia through the inauguration of Kia Spazio Tower, located in the West Surabaya area. The opening of this dealership is part of Kia’s strategy to expand its network and deliver Accessible Premium services across East Java.

Kia Spazio Tower is located at Jl. Mayjend. Jonosewojo Kav.3, Pradahkal, Surabaya, Graha Famili, West Surabaya, 60225, and is operated by Kia Azimut Group. The Kia Spazio Tower facility occupies an area of 199 m², consisting of both semi-outdoor and indoor areas. The dealership is designed to provide a more comfortable Kia product exploration experience for customers in Surabaya and East Java.

Jong Sung Park, CEO of Kia Sales Indonesia, said, “The opening of Kia Spazio is part of our long-term commitment to grow closer to Indonesian consumers, especially in Surabaya and East Java, which hold tremendous potential for Kia’s future growth. We want to ensure customers can enjoy greater comfort and more accessible access to the full Kia ownership experience, from the purchasing process to aftersales services,” he said.

Kia Strengthens Expansion and Growth Strategy in East Java

The presence of Kia Spazio Tower marks a strategic step in strengthening Kia’s network and expanding services in East Java, one of Indonesia’s growing automotive markets. This momentum also aligns with Kia’s repositioning strategy in Indonesia, which focuses on improving the accessibility of products and services for consumers. Kia will also participate in IIMS Surabaya 2026 as a strategic step to expand regional exposure, strengthen engagement with consumers in East Java, and reinforce Kia’s commitment to delivering increasingly accessible products and services.

Kia Spazio Tower Delivers Enhanced Services and Ownership Experience

Kia Spazio Tower is designed as a dealership that provides integrated services for Kia customers in Surabaya and East Java. The facility is equipped with a range of amenities designed to deliver comfort and convenience throughout the ownership journey, from vehicle purchasing to aftersales services, including a showroom area, customer lounge, dealing area, reception area, and other supporting facilities. Visitors can also experience Kia’s latest models directly through test drive sessions.

Kia Spazio Tower showcases a range of Kia flagship models, including the latest The All-New Carens and The New Sonet, both previously introduced at IIMS 2026.

Rudy Surjanto, President Director of Kia Azimut Group, stated that Surabaya continues to play an important role in Kia’s growth in East Java. “Surabaya continues to show growing demand for vehicles, especially in the SUV and MPV segments. Consumers today are placing greater emphasis on service quality and aftersales convenience. The presence of Kia Spazio Tower reflects our commitment to delivering services that are closer, more reliable, and more accessible for customers in East Java,” he concluded.
