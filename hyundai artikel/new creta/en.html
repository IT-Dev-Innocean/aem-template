Jakarta, June 9, 2026 – For many drivers, every journey brings different needs. A morning commute through city traffic calls for fuel efficiency, while a weekend trip with the family prioritizes comfort. At other times, steep mountain roads demand a more responsive engine. The ability of a vehicle to adapt to a variety of driving situations has therefore become one of the key considerations for today's drivers.

 

Addressing these needs, Hyundai introduces the New CRETA with a Drive Mode feature that can be adjusted directly via the Drive Mode Knob inside the cabin. With this feature, drivers no longer need to adapt to the vehicle's character, because the New CRETA adapts to the driver's needs instead.

 

 

How Does the New CRETA's Drive Mode Adapt to Every Journey?

 

The New CRETA offers four Drive Mode options, each designed to suit a different driving situation. Eco Mode is designed for drivers who prioritize efficiency in their daily commutes. With a smoother throttle response and engine revs kept at a lower level, this mode helps significantly reduce fuel consumption, whether crawling through city traffic or cruising steadily on the highway.

 

When the journey calls for a more relaxed pace, Normal Mode delivers a balance between power and efficiency, allowing all passengers to enjoy a more comfortable ride. This mode is suitable for a wide range of needs, from dropping the kids off at school to family outings on the weekend.

 

When the road gets challenging, such as long inclines on mountain highways or overtaking on a single-lane road, Sport Mode transforms the New CRETA's character into something more dynamic and responsive. The sharper throttle response gives the driver full control in every moment that demands decisiveness.

 

For drivers with busy schedules whose road conditions can change at any moment during a single trip, Smart Mode offers the most intuitive solution. This mode automatically reads the driving style and adjusts the vehicle's character in real time, functioning like Eco Mode during constant cruising, and immediately boosting responsiveness the moment the accelerator is pressed more firmly. One mode for all conditions.

 

 

How Does the New CRETA Maintain Performance Across Different Terrain?

 

The New CRETA's flexibility isn't limited to paved roads. The vehicle is also equipped with a Traction Control Mode offering three terrain-specific settings: Snow for slippery roads, Mud for muddy terrain, and Sand for sandy surfaces. With both Drive Mode and Traction Control Mode working in tandem, the New CRETA is able to maintain stability and control even in the most challenging road conditions.

 

The presence of both the Drive Mode and Traction Control Mode features reflects Hyundai's commitment to delivering a truly driver-centric vehicle, enabling every journey to be undertaken with greater confidence, comfort, and purpose.
