• ● Kia Seltos crowned ‘SUV of the Year’ by Times Drive, a leading automotive platform under Times Group, India’s largest media conglomerate.
  • ● The award reflects the all-new Seltos’s strong competitiveness in India’s B-SUV segment and Kia’s growing leadership in the global SUV market.
  • ● Recognition underscores Seltos’s balance of design, technology, safety, and comfort for everyday mobility.

 

SEOUL, 29 May 2026 – The all-new Kia Seltos has been named ‘SUV of the Year’ at the 2026 Times Drive Auto Summit & Awards in New Delhi, India, underscoring the model’s continued success in one of the world’s most dynamic and competitive automotive markets.

Organized by Times Drive, a leading automotive and mobility platform under Times Group, India’s largest media conglomerate, the awards recognize outstanding vehicles across the automotive industry.

The all-new Kia Seltos was selected by a panel of automotive journalists and media experts based on several evaluation criteria, including:

  • ● Design
  • ● Performance
  • ● Technology
  • ● Safety
  • ● Sustainability

This latest achievement highlights the all-new Seltos’s strong appeal in India’s highly competitive B-SUV segment, while reinforcing Kia’s growing brand presence and leadership in the global SUV market.

Ho Sung Song, President and CEO of Kia Corporation, said, “This recognition for the Kia Seltos reflects our continued commitment to delivering vehicles that combine design, technology and practicality in ways that resonate with our customers across diverse markets. As a core model in Kia’s global SUV lineup, Seltos plays a key role in strengthening our presence in strategic regions such as India. Accolades such as the 2026 Times Drive Auto Summit & Awards ‘SUV of the Year’ highlight the importance of delivering design-led, safety-focused, customer-centric mobility solutions that offer both everyday usability and long-term value.” He said.

 

Setting a New Standard in the Compact B-SUV Segment

The Seltos’s recognition reflects its broad range of capabilities and customer-focused features, combining authentic SUV design, advanced connectivity, and practical versatility tailored to a wide range of customer needs.

The judges noted the “love and appreciation received in the domestic market” when the Seltos made its debut, commenting that “The Seltos is well acknowledged for its design and feature-rich premium cabin.” The judging panel also highlighted that “the new Seltos boasts revised exterior and interior designs, including a dual display setup, and a suite of premium features, including a panoramic sunroof and advanced ADAS technology.”

Gwanggu Lee, Managing Director and CEO of Kia India, said, “The Seltos was developed with a clear intent from the very beginning – to understand Indian customers and build around their needs. With this new generation, we have taken that forward by making it more spacious, more confident in its design, stronger in safety and technology, and more aligned with how families use their vehicles. This recognition reflects the strength of that approach and the strong connect the Seltos continues to build with our customers.”

The new Kia Seltos achieved a 5-star Adult Safety and 5-star Child Safety rating from the Indian New Car Assessment Program (BNCAP).

As one of Kia’s key global models, Seltos continues to play a central role in Kia’s SUV strategy. The latest model has been developed with:

  • ● Enhanced interior space and improved everyday usability
  • ● Expanded capability across various driving conditions
  • ● Advanced technology and connectivity features

Together, these attributes deliver a confident and versatile driving experience tailored to diverse customer needs across global markets.

With its bold and distinctive SUV design, intelligent functionality, practicality, and strong overall value, the all-new Kia Seltos continues to set a benchmark in the compact SUV segment while supporting Kia’s broader ambition to deliver accessible and customer-centric mobility solutions worldwide.
