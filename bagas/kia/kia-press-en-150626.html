• ● Kia officially hands over its vehicle fleet to support FIFA World Cup 2026™ operations.
  • ● A total of 660 Kia vehicles will be deployed throughout the tournament across the United States, Canada, and Mexico.
  • ● Kia brings fans closer to the tournament through activations at Stadium Fan Experience and FIFA Fan Fest™.

 

SEOUL / LOS ANGELES, 15 June 2026 – As the FIFA World Cup 2026™ officially gets underway across the United States, Canada, and Mexico from June 11 to July 19, Kia is introducing a range of fan engagement activities while reinforcing its role as FIFA's Official Mobility Partner.

660 Vehicles to Support FIFA World Cup 2026™ Operations

As part of its global partnership with FIFA, Kia has officially handed over a fleet of 660 vehicles to support transportation and operational needs across host cities during the FIFA World Cup 2026™.

The fleet consists of vehicles from Kia's award-winning product lineup, including Telluride, Sportage, Carnival, Sorento, K4, K4 Hatchback, Niro, and Sonet. These vehicles will support a wide range of tournament operations across host markets, showcasing Kia's innovation, versatility, and mobility capabilities to a global audience.

Ho Sung Song, President and CEO of Kia Corporation, said, "Kia is honored to support the FIFA World Cup 2026™ as the Official Mobility Partner. This vehicle handover marks a significant milestone in our global partnership with FIFA and reflects Kia's commitment to delivering innovative and sustainable mobility solutions on the world stage. We look forward to contributing to creating a seamless and memorable tournament experience for all participants and fans worldwide."

The collaboration reflects Kia's brand philosophy of enabling meaningful movement by connecting people with inspiring experiences through mobility. Beyond the tournament itself, the partnership extends to fans and communities through additional initiatives, including the launch of Kia's exclusive FIFA World Cup 2026™ Display Themes and the hosting of young players from around the world through the Kia OMBC Cup 2026™.

Bringing Fans Together Through FIFA Fan Fest™ and Stadium Fan Experience

In addition to supporting tournament mobility, Kia is bringing its global campaign, "Inspiration Connects Us All," to life through a series of fan engagement activities across Stadium Fan Experience and FIFA Fan Fest™ locations throughout the host countries.

Minsoo Kim, Executive Vice President and Head of Global Brand & CX Division, Kia Corporation, said, "The FIFA World Cup 2026™ is a global stage where Kia is redefining how fans connect through football and mobility. Through bold, immersive experiences, we are bringing our brand vision 'Inspiration Connects Us All' to life while creating deeper and more meaningful connections that resonate with fans around the world."

Kia has also launched its FIFA World Cup 2026™ Hero Film, which follows the journey of a young player stepping onto football's biggest stage. Building on Kia's Official Match Ball Carrier (OMBC) program and the Kia OMBC Cup 2026™, the film highlights moments of anticipation, excitement, and inspiration experienced by young players from around the world as they become part of the FIFA World Cup™.

Throughout the tournament, Kia will introduce a variety of interactive fan experiences at Stadium Fan Experience and FIFA Fan Fest™ locations across host cities.

These activations include the Custom Player Card Experience, where fans can create personalized digital player cards featuring themselves wearing their national team uniform. Kia will also showcase Country & City-Themed Vehicle Displays, featuring vehicles wrapped in custom designs inspired by participating nations and host cities. In addition, the Tri-Net Themed Architecture will serve as a signature fan engagement space, symbolizing the first-ever FIFA World Cup™ hosted by three nations and providing visitors with opportunities to interact, connect, and share memorable moments.

Kia will further support fan mobility through dedicated shuttle services in Los Angeles and Vancouver during the tournament. The shuttle fleet will include Telluride, Carnival, EV9, and EV5 models, helping fans travel conveniently between key event locations while enhancing the overall tournament experience.

As FIFA's Official Mobility Partner, Kia will continue supporting tournament operations through the deployment of 660 vehicles across the United States, Canada, and Mexico, while delivering mobility solutions that connect fans, players, and communities throughout the FIFA World Cup 2026™.

Kia has been an official partner of FIFA since 2007. As FIFA's Official Mobility Partner, Kia will provide transportation, autonomous driving technologies, and future mobility services across FIFA events and tournaments through 2030.
