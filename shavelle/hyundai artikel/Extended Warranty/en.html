• Extended Warranty Program Hyundai is available for various models such as the CRETA, STARGAZER, and SANTA FE, offering an extension of official warranty coverage and Roadside Assistance for 1 year or 15,000 km after the standard warranty period ends.
  • Available in two package options, Extended Warranty and Extended Warranty+, the program offers flexible protection tailored to the needs of each vehicle owner, with prices starting from Rp600,000.
  • All Extended Warranty packages are available at authorized Hyundai workshops across Indonesia, subject to the vehicle's standard warranty remaining active and the vehicle having undergone regular scheduled servicing.

 

Jakarta, June 15, 2026 – For vehicle owners, the longer they have owned their car, the more their priorities around maintenance and protection tend to shift. As a vehicle ages, the need for reliable after-sales protection becomes increasingly relevant, while service and repair costs can grow significantly harder to predict once the standard warranty period comes to an end.

 

In response to this need, Hyundai introduces the Extended Warranty Program as part of its myHyundaiCare after-sales service commitment. The program is designed to provide additional protection beyond the standard warranty period, so that vehicle owners can continue to enjoy their journeys with confidence and greater peace of mind.

 

 

What Does the Hyundai Extended Warranty Program Offer?

 

The Extended Warranty Program is available across a range of Hyundai models, including CRETA, STARGAZER, and SANTA FE, with prices starting from Rp600,000. The program gives vehicle owners the flexibility to choose the level of protection that best suits their needs.

 

The Extended Warranty package covers:

 

  • An extension of Hyundai's official warranty for 1 year or 15,000 km, covering the repair and/or replacement of damaged genuine Hyundai parts as well as repair costs arising from assembly errors and/or production defects.
  • An extension of Roadside Assistance (RSA) services for 1 year or 15,000 km, including towing, spare tyre replacement, battery jump-start, 5 litres of emergency fuel, and lockout assistance.

 

Exclusively for CRETA and STARGAZER owners, the Extended Warranty+ package is also available, offering all the above benefits plus one complimentary Periodic Maintenance service covering both labor and parts costs in accordance with the vehicle's maintenance guidelines.

Who Is Eligible for the Extended Warranty Program?

 

The Extended Warranty Program is open to owners of various Hyundai models who meet the following requirements:

 

  • The vehicle's standard warranty remains active and has not been voided
  • The vehicle remains in factory-standard condition with no modifications affecting performance or safety
  • The vehicle has consistently used genuine Hyundai parts and undergone regular Periodic Maintenance at an authorized Hyundai workshop
  • The vehicle has not previously been enrolled in a similar Extended Warranty program

 

The Extended Warranty Program reflects Hyundai's commitment to accompanying customers throughout the full vehicle ownership cycle, not only at the point of purchase. Through the myHyundaiCare service ecosystem, Hyundai delivers a comprehensive ownership experience from the first purchase through to resale.
