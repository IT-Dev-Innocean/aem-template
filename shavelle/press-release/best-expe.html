  • ● The All-New Carens and The New Sonet return as the main highlights, continuing their momentum following their debut at IIMS 2026
  • ● Kia Total Care program, including a 7 Year Warranty or 200,000 km and 4 Year Free Maintenance, strengthens customer confidence and ownership cost certainty

 

Jakarta, 8 May 2026 – Kia once again presents a series of exhibitions through Kia Experience Week, held throughout May 2026 across several shopping malls in various cities in Indonesia. This program is part of Kia’s effort to bring product exploration experiences closer and make them more relevant, featuring two key models, The All-New Carens and The New Sonet, designed to meet the mobility needs of modern families and urban lifestyles.

“Kia Experience Week is designed to respond to this need by bringing our products directly closer to where customers carry out their daily activities across Jabodetabek and surrounding areas, making the interaction feel more relevant and personal,” said Harry Yanto, VP Operations of Kia Sales Indonesia.

Expanding Consumer Reach Through Mall-to-Mall Activation and Kia Total Care Program

Through Kia Experience Week, Kia showcases a range of models including The Kia Carnival and The Kia EV9 GT Line. The Kia Carnival continues to be positioned as a benchmark for comfort for families with high mobility, with space configuration and features designed for long-distance travel. Meanwhile, The Kia EV9 GT-Line represents Kia’s electrification direction, bringing electric vehicle technology into an SUV format that aligns with Indonesian consumers who prioritize space, performance, and efficiency.

Kia also features its latest MPV and SUV, The All-New Carens and The New Sonet, which were previously introduced at the Indonesia International Motor Show 2026. The All-New Carens enters the MPV segment with flexible 6- and 7-seater configurations, targeting young families who need space, comfort, and efficiency for daily use as well as long-distance travel. Meanwhile, The New Sonet competes in the highly competitive compact SUV segment, offering a compact size, strong driving visibility, and features that support dynamic urban mobility, making it relevant both as a first car and as an upgrade option for consumers in major cities.

Kia also introduces the Kia Total Care program, specifically offered for The All-New Carens and The New Sonet. This program includes a 7 Year Warranty or up to 200,000 km* and 4 Year Free Maintenance*, providing greater certainty and strengthening customer confidence in the quality and durability of Kia products.

“Through Kia Experience Week, we want to create space for consumers to truly get to know our products without pressure, with sufficient time and easier access. We believe strong purchase decisions come from a complete understanding, not just a brief impression. That is why we come directly into their daily spaces, while ensuring every product we offer is supported by clear and reliable long-term services,” Harry concluded.
