• ● Kia EV9 GT ranked first in Auto Bild Germany’s comparative test, reinforcing its competitiveness in the global electric SUV segment
  • ● A design approach focused on ease of use and intuitive operation emerged as a key differentiator in the independent evaluation
  • ● The balance of performance, flexibility, and everyday usability establishes the EV9 GT as a new benchmark in its class

 

Jakarta, 22 April 2026 – Kia continues to demonstrate its direction in premium electric vehicle development through the Kia EV9 GT, which secured first place in a comparative test conducted by leading German automotive publication, Auto Bild. This achievement signals that new standards in the electric SUV segment are no longer defined solely by performance, but by a vehicle’s ability to deliver an experience that is intuitive, comfortable, and relevant to everyday use. The result comes from a head-to-head evaluation against multiple competitors in its class, further reinforcing the EV9 GT’s global competitiveness.

This recognition reflects Kia’s approach in developing the EV9 GT, focusing not only on high performance, but also on how technology, design, and functionality can be seamlessly integrated into daily use. As consumer expectations toward electric vehicles continue to evolve, the EV9 GT represents a more comprehensive standard for modern mobility.

“Recognition from a publication like Auto Bild shows that in highly mature markets such as Germany, evaluation standards are increasingly centered on user experience, not just specifications. This is becoming relevant across markets, where electric vehicles are expected to be not only advanced, but also easy to use and comfortable in various situations,” said Rendy Pratama, Head of Marketing of Kia Sales Indonesia.

Design and Features as the Foundation of a Modern Electric SUV

The EV9 GT’s win in Auto Bild’s comparative test is rooted in a design philosophy that places ease of use at the core of the driving experience. In the premium electric SUV segment, this approach is becoming a key differentiator, as many vehicles grow increasingly complex in operation.

The EV9 GT takes a different direction by maintaining physical controls for essential functions, allowing drivers to operate key features without relying entirely on layered screen navigation. During testing, most functions could be accessed within two to three steps, enabling a more direct interaction without disrupting driving focus.

This approach is supported by integrated technology designed to be intuitive from the outset, including a 30-inch integrated digital display and advanced driver assistance systems that enhance usability. Control placement within the driver’s natural reach further minimizes distraction, especially during long-distance travel.

In its evaluation, Auto Bild highlighted the balance between ease of use, comfort, and performance as the defining factor that sets the EV9 GT apart from its competitors. This reinforces the role of design not only as a visual element, but as a key contributor to driving safety and comfort.

Proven Performance and Flexibility in Real-World Use

The EV9 GT does not pursue performance as a standalone figure, but focuses on delivering controlled and consistent power across various driving conditions. Equipped with a dual motor system producing 508 PS, it accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 4.3 seconds, based on Auto Bild testing. Beyond the numbers, the acceleration remains smooth and manageable, ensuring comfort in both daily driving and long-distance journeys.

Testing also showed stable handling across different road conditions. The chassis effectively absorbs surface changes without compromising control, while steering response remains precise and easy to adapt to from the first drive. This combination creates a confident driving experience without requiring excessive adjustment from the driver.

In terms of flexibility, the EV9 GT is designed for practical use. It offers six- and seven-seat configurations, with a third row that remains fully usable. Access is convenient, space is sufficient, and comfort is maintained even during longer trips.

Efficiency is also part of the overall experience. Supported by an 800V architecture, the battery can be charged from 10 to 80 percent in approximately 24 minutes using a 350 kW DC fast charger. Cargo capacity reaches up to 2,393 liters with the seats folded, enhancing versatility for various needs. The EV9 GT also features Vehicle-to-Load (V2L) capability of up to 3.6 kW, allowing it to function as an external power source when needed.

A Relevant Proposition for Premium Electric SUV Consumers

Auto Bild’s evaluation shows that the EV9 GT’s appeal does not rely on a single attribute, but on consistency across multiple aspects that matter most to consumers in this segment. Design, interior functionality, charging performance, and ease of use are no longer assessed separately, but as a unified experience.

The EV9 GT addresses this with a more measured approach. Users can quickly understand the vehicle’s logic without a long learning curve, an increasingly important factor as many electric vehicles adopt more complex interfaces. This ease of use becomes immediately apparent in everyday driving.

At the same time, the product identity remains strong through a bold and proportional design, supported by 21-inch GT-style wheels and signature lighting elements that reinforce its modern character. The interior offers solid material quality and flexible space configuration, ensuring the vehicle feels premium while remaining highly functional.

“As electric vehicles continue to evolve, consumer expectations are becoming clearer. What matters is not only technology, but how easy the vehicle is to use, how comfortable it feels in different situations, and how efficiently it supports daily life. The EV9 GT shows that when these elements are well integrated, the driving experience is not only impressive on paper, but truly felt in every journey,” concluded Rendy.
