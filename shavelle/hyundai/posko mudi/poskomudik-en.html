• HMID presents the Hyundai Mudik Program 2026 to ensure customers enjoy a safer, more comfortable, and worry-free homecoming journey.
  • The program includes Hyundai Before ServiceTM at KM57 Cikampek Rest Area and 24- hour Emergency Services consisting of Mobile Service, Mobile Charging, Take Me Home, and Free Towing Service, all integrated with Hyundai’s nationwide authorized workshop network.
  • Hyundai has also prepared 24 Standby Workshops that will remain operational during the Eid holiday period at strategic locations.

 

Jakarta, 17 March 2026 – PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID) once again presents the Hyundai Mudik Program 2026 to ensure customers can enjoy a safer, more comfortable, and worry-free homecoming journey during the Eid travel period.

 

Fransiscus Soerjopranoto, Chief Operating Officer PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia, said, “myHyundaiCare reflects our commitment not only to delivering innovative vehicles, but also to providing peace of mind for our customers. We bring this commitment to life through responsive and comprehensive aftersales services, especially during critical moments such as the annual homecoming season.”

 

“Our services are available for all Hyundai vehicles, whether they are still under warranty or already out of warranty. We want to ensure every customer can travel with confidence and comfort.” he added.

 

Through myHyundaiCare, Hyundai delivers various customer support initiatives, including Hyundai Before Service™ at KM57 Rest Area, which functions as both a rest stop and a vehicle inspection and repair point. This is supported by 24-hour emergency services ranging from free towing and mobile service to Hyundai’s flagship services such as Hyundai Mobile Charging Service and Hyundai Take Me Home. In addition, Hyundai Standby Workshops across several cities will remain open during the Eid holiday period to ensure customers can continue their journeys safely and comfortably.

 

How Does Hyundai Ensure Vehicles Are Ready for the Homecoming Journey?

 

A safe and comfortable homecoming trip depends greatly on the condition of the vehicle. To support this, Hyundai presents the Hyundai Before Service™ booth at KM57 Cikampek Rest Area, operating 24 hours a day from March 17–23, 2026.

 

Through this facility, Hyundai provides a range of complimentary services and special benefits, including:

 

  • Vehicle Maintenance: Free vehicle inspection and service, including free car wash and air/nitrogen tire refill.
  • Oil & Spare Parts Support: Free 2 liters of engine oil (for oil changes conducted on site) and a 20% discount voucher for spare parts.
  • Emergency Repair & EV Charging: Emergency repair services and AC charging facilities for Hyundai electric vehicle (EV) users.

 

Through the presence of Hyundai Before ServiceTM, customers can ensure their vehicles are in optimal condition, allowing them to travel home more safely and comfortably.

 

What Support Does Hyundai Provide for Situations During the Journey?

 

In addition to Hyundai Before Service™, Hyundai also provides 24-hour Emergency Services during the homecoming and return travel period from March 18 to March 29, 2026.

 

These services are available for all Hyundai customers, whether their vehicles are still under warranty or already out of warranty. The comprehensive support includes:

 

  • Towing Service: Free towing service connected to Hyundai’s nationwide authorized workshop network.
  • Hyundai Mobile Service: On-site emergency repair services available in strategic regions including Greater Jakarta, Cirebon, Bandung, Semarang, Yogyakarta, Medan, and Bali.
  • Hyundai Mobile Charging: Emergency Vehicle-to-Vehicle charging support for Hyundai electric vehicle users traveling through Jakarta, Bandung, Semarang, Yogyakarta, Surabaya, Bali, and Medan.
  • Hyundai Take Me Home: Customer transportation service within the city in collaboration with Grab Indonesia, provided if the vehicle needs to be towed to a workshop.

 

All emergency services can be accessed through the Hyundai 24-Hour Call Center (toll-free) at 0 800 1 878 878, ensuring customers receive quick and responsive assistance during their homecoming journey.

 

Where Are Hyundai Standby Workshops Available During the Mudik Period?

 

As part of the Hyundai Mudik Program 2026, Hyundai will also operate 24 Hyundai Standby Workshops during the Eid holiday period from March 19–24, 2026. All Hyundai authorized workshops will resume normal operations on March 25, 2026.

 

These standby workshops are located across strategic areas in Java, Bali, and Sumatra, ensuring customers can easily access vehicle services when needed during their journeys.
