• Hyundai Motors Indonesia invites certain all-new PALISADE Hybrid customers to perform an Instrument Panel (IP) software update at authorized dealers to ensure optimal driving information and support safety throughout the journey.
  • All updates will be carried out at authorized Hyundai dealers and are provided free of charge for customers.

 

Jakarta, 19 May 2026 – PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID) continues to demonstrate its commitment to delivering a safe and comfortable driving experience for customers. As part of its ongoing efforts to maintain vehicle quality and performance, Hyundai invites certain all-new PALISADE Hybrid customers to visit authorized Hyundai dealers for inspection and software update of the Instrument Panel (IP).

 

This initiative reflects Hyundai’s proactive approach to ensuring that the vehicle’s information system functions optimally under various driving conditions. With a well-maintained instrument panel, customers can continue to access clear and reliable driving information throughout their journey.

 

The software update focuses on optimizing the Instrument Panel system, where under certain conditions, the display may experience temporary disruption due to technical factors. Through this software update, the system will be enhanced to operate more stably and consistently in presenting essential information for the driver.

 

The software update process will be carried out at authorized Hyundai dealers with an estimated completion time of approximately 36 minutes, and customers will not incur any charges for this service. In accordance with applicable regulations in Indonesia, HMID has informed customers through various communication channels and through Hyundai’s official website. In addition, Hyundai is proactively reaching out to customers to provide reminders and ensure a smooth update process. Customers can also obtain further information 24/7 through the Hyundai Call Center at 0 800 1 878 878 (toll-free).

 

Hyundai places customer safety and comfort as a top priority through this initiative, ensuring that every customer can continue to enjoy an optimal driving experience supported by a reliable vehicle information system. The company will also continue to monitor and implement necessary improvements as part of its commitment to providing peace of mind for customers.
