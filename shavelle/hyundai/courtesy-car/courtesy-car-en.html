• Hyundai introduces the Premium Courtesy Car Program as part of its myHyundaiCare commitment to ensure customers’ mobility remains uninterrupted while their vehicles undergo service or repairs at authorized Hyundai workshops.
  • This program positions Hyundai as a pioneer in Indonesia’s automotive industry in delivering an integrated courtesy car service for customers.
  • Hyundai has prepared more than 250 courtesy car units across various regions in Indonesia.
  • The program continues to be developed to serve a broader range of customer needs, in line with Hyundai’s customer-focused commitment.

 

Jakarta, 16 April 2026 – PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia continues to affirm its commitment to enhancing the quality of its aftersales services by strengthening the Hyundai Premium Courtesy Car Program, a replacement vehicle service designed to ensure customers can continue their daily activities comfortably while their vehicles undergo the service process at authorized Hyundai workshops.

 

This program is part of the myHyundaiCare service ecosystem, developed to deliver a more seamless, comfortable, and worry-free vehicle ownership experience. This service also reflects the implementation of Hyundai's global commitment to improving aftersales service quality. In Indonesia, Hyundai is among the leading companies in offering courtesy car services, supported by the wide availability of premium vehicles across its dealer network to help ensure customer comfort remains well maintained.

 

To support customers' convenience during the vehicle repair process, Hyundai provides replacement vehicles including the premium electric vehicle Hyundai IONIQ 5, subject to availability at each respective dealer. Notably, customers with ICE (Internal Combustion Engine) vehicles are also given the opportunity to use the IONIQ 5 — where available — as part of Hyundai's effort to provide customers with an electric vehicle (EV) driving experience.

 

Kenny Lee, President Director of Hyundai Motors Indonesia said, “Hyundai is committed to ensuring our customers’ mobility and peace of mind. By introducing the Hyundai Premium Courtesy Car Program and preparing more than 250 vehicles at our selected dealer network, we make sure customers can continue their daily activities smoothly, even when their cars require extended service. This initiative is a tangible expression of Hyundai's commitment to continuously improving service quality and delivering the best possible experience for our customers.”

 

"Hyundai Premium Courtesy Car Program ensures customers stay mobile while their vehicles are serviced overnight. By offering models such as Hyundai IONIQ 5, we provide practical convenience and, at the same time, introduce more customers to Hyundai’s electric mobility. This reflects our commitment as a customer-centric brand and to continuously improving aftersales service quality in Indonesia." said Fransiscus Soerjopranoto, Chief Operating Officer of Hyundai Motors Indonesia.

 

How can customers access this service?

 

The Hyundai Premium Courtesy Car Program is available to all Hyundai customers through Hyundai's authorized workshop network across Indonesia. The program applies to all vehicles distributed by PT Hyundai Motor Indonesia, with no restrictions on vehicle age or mileage.

 

The main requirements of the program include:

  • The vehicle is undergoing general repair with an estimated service time of more than 1 x 24 hours;
  • The vehicle is undergoing body and paint repair with a duration of more than 14 x 24 hours;
  • The maximum loan period is up to 17 days, with possible extensions subject to the customer's needs and dealer approval;
  • Replacement vehicles may include Hyundai models such as the IONIQ 5, CRETA, STARGAZER, or other models depending on availability at each dealer.

 

Through this scheme, Hyundai ensures that the Premium Courtesy Car Program can be delivered optimally, remain relevant, and provide tangible benefits to customers in various situations. This program is supported by participating Hyundai dealerships across various regions in Indonesia and will continue to expand gradually to reach more customers.
