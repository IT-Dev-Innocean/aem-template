• Hyundai Indonesia is implementing a software update for the all-new PALISADE Hybrid to ensure optimal vehicle performance while enhancing customer comfort and safety.
  • The software update is carried out via Over-the-Air (OTA), allowing customers to easily update their vehicles without visiting a dealer, supported by Hyundai’s proactive communication as a reminder and to ensure a smooth update process.

 

Jakarta, 19 May 2026 – PT Hyundai Motors Indonesia (HMID) once again reaffirms its commitment to ensuring customer safety and comfort through a software update for the all-new PALISADE Hybrid units in Indonesia, specifically related to the second- and third-row power seats. Following this update, customers can enjoy a smoother and more reliable seat folding and unfolding experience in both rows, providing greater comfort for all passengers on every journey.

 

Hyundai places customer safety and comfort as a top priority through this software update, ensuring that every all-new PALISADE Hybrid customer can continue to enjoy an optimal, seamless, and worry-free driving experience. The company will also continue to monitor and implement necessary improvements regularly as part of its commitment to providing peace of mind for customers.

 

The software update can be performed via Over-the-Air (OTA), a wireless update system that enables vehicles to receive updates directly without the need to visit a dealer. With this technology, customers can carry out updates more easily and conveniently, similar to updating digital devices. The update process takes approximately 18 minutes and is completely free of charge.

 

The update process is also designed to be seamless, as a notification will automatically appear on the vehicle’s head unit when the update is available. Customers are advised to have sufficient time before starting the installation process, as the vehicle cannot be operated during the update.

 

In accordance with applicable regulations in Indonesia, HMID has informed customers through various communication channels and Hyundai’s official website. In addition, Hyundai is also proactively reaching out to customers as a reminder and to ensure the software update process can be completed smoothly.

 

Customers can also obtain further information 24/7 through the Hyundai Call Center at 0 800 1 878 878 (toll-free).
