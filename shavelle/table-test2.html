How to Install:
• Insert the locking key of the holder into the slot on the dashboard panel and rotate upward.
• Loosen the locking nut, insert the ball joint, and push the holder onto the joint until secure.
• Adjust the angle for optimal viewing and tighten the nut to lock it in place.
• Connect the charging cable to the holder and the vehicle's power source.
Precaution:
• Ensure the holder is firmly locked into the dashboard socket before attaching your iPhone.
• Do not over-tighten when rotating to prevent damage to the socket or locking mechanism.
• Use only MagSafe-compatible iPhones or certified MagSafe cases to ensure safe charging.
• Keep the charging surface clean and dry to maintain optimal magnetic strength and charging efficiency.
• Avoid placing metal objects or non-MagSafe accessories between the holder and the phone.
• Periodically check the cable connection and holder stability, especially after long drives or exposure to heat.
Item Required: • Ball joint & holder (included in package)
