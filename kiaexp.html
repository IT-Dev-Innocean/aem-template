• ● Kia Experience Week continues throughout June 2026, expanding its reach to shopping malls and strategic locations across Indonesia.
  • ● Consumers can explore Kia’s latest line-up, including The all-new Carens, The new Sonet, The Kia Carnival, and The Kia EV9.
  • ● Kia Total Care further enhances ownership confidence through aftersales programs designed to provide greater convenience and peace of mind.

 

Jakarta, 10 June 2026 – Kia continues its Kia Experience Week series throughout June 2026 with activations across a number of shopping centers and strategic locations in various cities across Indonesia. The program reflects Kia’s commitment to bringing its products and brand experience closer to consumers, while providing more opportunities for the public to explore Kia’s vehicle lineup firsthand.

“Through the June edition of Kia Experience Week, we are also expanding into several new locations that were not previously part of the program. This allows more consumers across different cities to experience Kia’s products and services firsthand and engage with the brand more closely,” said Harry Yanto, VP Operations of Kia Sales Indonesia.

Throughout Kia Experience Week, Kia is showcasing a range of its key products, with particular focus on The all-new Carens and The new Sonet. Both models recently received positive market response during IIMS Surabaya 2026 in May. Through this program, Kia aims to make these products more accessible to consumers in various regions across the country.

Kia is also displaying other flagship models, including The Kia Carnival, a premium MPV available with hybrid and diesel powertrains, and The Kia EV9, a luxury EV-SUV that represents Kia’s commitment to electrification and sustainable mobility solutions.

In addition, Kia continues to offer the Kia Total Care program, available exclusively for The all-new Carens and The new Sonet. The program is designed to provide greater ownership confidence while reinforcing Kia’s commitment to quality, reliability, and long-term customer satisfaction.

Throughout June 2026, Kia Experience Week will be held at the following locations:

  • ● June 1–28: Panakuang Mall, Makassar
  • ● June 8–15: 23 Paskal Shopping Center, Bandung
  • ● June 9–14: Pakuwon Mall Jogja
  • ● June 9–14: Duta Mall Banjarmasin
  • ● June 15–21: Margo City, Depok
  • ● June 15–21: Plaza Balikpapan
  • ● June 15–22: Pakuwon Mall Surabaya

“We want every visitor to feel that Kia is here to support their mobility needs through products that are relevant and services they can rely on. Going forward, we will continue expanding our reach and exploring new ways to engage more closely with consumers across Indonesia,” Harry concluded.
